PORTFOLIO

Challenges

- Shopify is shopping app for people to buy items remotely.
- Users can buy any convenient items they want on the website.

Objectives

- Ensure users feel welcome and satisfy with their purchases.
- Provide quality and cheap items.

         Persona



- Fictional users whose goals and characteristics represent the needs of a larger group of users.
- This persona become the key to the design process because it reflects the lifestyles of users and give my team an idea of how to meet users‚Äô needs or challenges.
- The design ensures users to have the best experience while using the product since these information speak directly to users.

         Sticky Notes



- Based on the theme that: Most participants feel that the backgorund color doesn't match what the website looks like. An insight is: users feel relieved since the photo on the websites looks pleasure and trustable to what the page is.
- Based on the theme that: Most participants are confused if there are discount or shipping options. An insight is: users can create shipping and discounting buttons for users to choose.
- Based on the theme that: Many participants can't decided which items have best customer services. An insight is: users are surprised that they can sort out price and choose recommended items.

         Digital Wireframes

- Representation of a final product that demonstrates its functionality.
- A series of interconnected mockups that represent all the pages in a design.
- Demonstrates how the products look, feel and work before passing it down to the development team.
